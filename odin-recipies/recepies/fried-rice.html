Veg Fried Rice

Description

Quick stir-fried rice with colorful veggies and soy sauce.

Best with day-old rice for ideal texture.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice (cold)
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, bell pepper)
  • Garlic, spring onions
  • Soy sauce, oil, pepper
  • (Optional) scrambled egg or tofu

Steps

  1. Heat oil; sauté garlic and veggies.
  2. Add rice; stir-fry on high heat.
  3. Season with soy sauce and pepper.
  4. Toss in egg/tofu if using; finish with spring onions.
