Classic Lasagna

Description

Layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese baked to bubbly perfection.

Great for family dinners; leftovers taste even better the next day.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or plant-based mince
  • Tomato sauce & garlic
  • Ricotta, mozzarella, parmesan
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Boil noodles until al dente.
  2. Cook mince with garlic; add tomato sauce and simmer.
  3. Layer sauce, noodles, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 190°C (375°F) for ~30–40 minutes.
  5. Rest 10 minutes, slice, and serve.
