Fluffy Pancakes

Description

Light, airy pancakes perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Serve with butter, maple syrup, and fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • Flour, baking powder, sugar, salt
  • Milk and eggs
  • Butter (melted) or oil
  • Vanilla (optional)

Steps

  1. Whisk dry ingredients in one bowl, wet in another.
  2. Combine gently; a few lumps are fine.
  3. Cook on a hot greased pan until bubbles form; flip.
  4. Serve warm with toppings.
